Outer Space: A World of Mysteries

Beyond our atmosphere extends a vast universe containing billions of galaxies, stars, and planets. Explore with us worlds that might be habitable outside our blue planet.

Potentially Habitable Planets

Mars

The Red Planet, our closest neighbor in the solar system. Features water ice at its poles and seasons similar to Earth. Scientists are targeting manned missions in the coming decades.

See More

Earth

The Blue Planet, our only known home in the universe. Features an oxygen-rich atmosphere, vast amounts of liquid water, and unique biodiversity that supports life in all its forms.

See More

Kepler-186f

The first Earth-sized planet discovered in the habitable zone of another star. Located about 500 light-years away and orbits a red dwarf star, making it a strong candidate for hosting life.
